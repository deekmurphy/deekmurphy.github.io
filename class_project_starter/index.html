Diana is Digital Production Coordinator on the Web and Digital Strategy team at the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco. She holds an MA in Museum Studies from Marist College, Istituto Lorenzo de'Medici, Florence focusing on contemporary museum architecture and gallery spaces. She received her BA in the History of Art and Visual Culture from the University of California, Santa Cruz in 2010 and participated in the Goldsmiths, University of London and the British School at Rome curatorial intensive this past summer.
